Types of trade licenses

Professionals Licence

Commercial or Trade Licences

In Dubai, trade licenses are required for everyone engaged in trade, including importers, exporters, vehicle rental agencies, and real estate developers

Industrial Licence

General Trade Licenses

General trade licenses are required to conduct general trades such as imports and exports.
